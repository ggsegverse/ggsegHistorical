# Source Data

The FreeSurfer annotation files in `fsaverage5/` were derived from the
supplementary materials of:

> Pijnenburg R, Scholtens LH, Mantini D, de Reus MA, van den Heuvel MP (2021).
> Myelo- and cytoarchitectonic microstructural and functional human cortical
> atlases reconstructed in common MRI space. *NeuroImage*, 239, 118274.
> <https://doi.org/10.1016/j.neuroimage.2021.118274>

## Download

The original atlas collection can be downloaded from the Dutch Connectome Lab:

<http://www.dutchconnectomelab.nl/assets/Pijnenburg_et_al_2021_NeuroImage.zip>

The zip contains `AtlasCollection/` with subdirectories for each atlas
(Brodmann, Campbell, EconomoCT, Flechsig, Kleist, Smith). Each contains:

- `{lh,rh}.atlas.gcs` — FreeSurfer surface-based atlas classifiers
- `{lh,rh}.fsaverage_{Name}.label.gii` — surface atlas on fsaverage
- `{Name}.nii.gz` / `{Name}_ICBM152.nii.gz` — volume-based atlases
- `{Name}ColorLUT.txt` / `{lh,rh}.colortable.txt` — colour tables
- `labels/` — individual manually drawn labels
- `README_{Name}.txt` — region descriptions (copies kept in `source-docs/`)

## Regenerating annotation files

The `.annot` files in `fsaverage5/` were generated by applying the `.gcs`
atlas classifiers to FreeSurfer's fsaverage subject and resampling to
fsaverage5 (10,242 vertices per hemisphere). This requires FreeSurfer
installed with `mris_ca_label` and `mri_surf2surf`.
